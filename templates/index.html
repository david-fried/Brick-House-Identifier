First, the bricklaying

Let’s say you’re in the masonry business. To target sales for home repairs, you may need to determine brick homes from non-brick homes. In the context of machine learning, categorization is a classification problem. To make predictions on unseen data, a training dataset must be designed. A training dataset is pre-classified through a manual or semi-automated process.

We retrieved image data primarily from a python script that interfaced with the Google Streetview API. By collecting data through Google Streetview, we were able to design a model specific to the Evanston, Illinois area and link our data directly to geocoded information. To ensure our model had more distinct versions of brick and non-brick homes, additional data was scraped from Google Images. All collected images were sorted between brick, siding, and unknown.
