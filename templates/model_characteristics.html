Tuning the model


Our model utilizes a convolutional neural network to learn and recognize brick homes. A convolutional neural network (CNN/ConvNet) learns weights and biases from an input image. CNNs have become dominant in various deep learning and computer vision tasks due to their ability to quickly learn spatial hierarchies.
